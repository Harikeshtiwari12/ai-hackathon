00 — The Why

AI isn't coming. It's already here. Are we ready?

Here's the uncomfortable truth: AI is already changing how our competitors work. While we debate whether to "explore AI," other companies are using it to write proposals in 5 minutes, resolve tickets before lunch, and prep demos that feel personalized — because they are.

But here's the good news: AI literacy isn't a talent. It's a skill. And like any skill, it can be learned through consistent, small practice.

That's why we're running the Couch to 5K for AI — a proven 30-day program (featured in Harvard Business Review) created by Lenny Rachitsky and Hilary Gridley. It's designed for people who keep saying "I should really learn AI" but never find the time.

10 minutes a day. That's it. Each day builds on the last. By Day 30, you won't just "know about AI" — you'll be using it to do your actual job faster and better.

This isn't optional enthusiasm. This is TranZact investing in every single person's future.

02 — The 4-Week Journey

From "What's a prompt?" to "I automated my Monday."

Each week has a clear theme and builds on the last. You'll never feel lost — and you'll never feel bored.

🌱 Week 1 — First Steps
~10 min/day
Theme: Learn to talk to AI
You'll learn how to have a real conversation with AI — not just "write me an email" but structured prompts that give you genuinely useful output. By Friday, you'll be getting answers that feel like they came from a smart colleague, not a robot.
Your first AI conversation Writing better prompts Giving AI context & role Iterating on responses Summarizing real documents
Outcome: You can use AI to draft emails, summarize notes, brainstorm ideas, and get answers 10x faster than Googling.
🔧 Week 2 — Daily Workflows
~10 min/day
Theme: Apply AI to your actual job
Now it gets real. You'll take your daily tasks — the ones you do every single day — and find where AI can cut the time in half. Sales reps will prep demos faster. CX folks will draft customer responses in seconds. Engineers will debug smarter.
AI for emails & comms Meeting prep & follow-ups Data analysis with AI Customer response drafting Research & competitive intel
Outcome: You've identified 3+ daily tasks where AI saves you real time. You're already faster than you were two weeks ago.
🚀 Week 3 — Build & Automate
~10 min/day
Theme: From chatting to building
This is where the magic happens. You'll move beyond chatting with AI to actually building things — simple automations, connected workflows, tools that run while you sleep. No coding required. The program uses no-code tools that anyone can pick up.
AI-powered templates Simple automations Connecting AI to your tools Building reusable prompts Creating AI assistants
Outcome: You've built at least one automation that saves you time every week. You're not just using AI — you're building with it.
🏆 Week 4 — Level Up & Share
~10 min/day
Theme: Teach, share, and make it stick
The final week is about solidifying your skills and sharing what you've learned. You'll tackle advanced use cases, help your teammates, and present one "AI win" to the team. The best way to learn is to teach — and this is where it all clicks.
Advanced prompting Multi-step AI workflows Teaching a teammate Your "AI Win" showcase Building an AI habit
Outcome: You've presented one real AI win to the team. You have a daily AI habit. You're now the person others come to for "how did you do that?"
03 — What's In It For You

This isn't generic training. It's built for how you work.

Whether you're in Sales, CX, Engineering, or Marketing — the exercises adapt to your role. Here's what each team will walk away with:

💰 Sales Team

Demo Prep in Minutes

AI that researches the prospect, builds a personalized talk track, and highlights the exact features that matter to their industry. Walk into every demo over-prepared.

Intent-Based Outreach

Learn to use AI for smarter follow-ups — detect buying signals in conversations and trigger the right message at the right moment. No more spray-and-pray.

Pipeline Intelligence

Use AI to analyze your pipeline, flag at-risk deals, and prioritize who to call today. Your CRM becomes a co-pilot, not a chore.

🤝 CX & Support

Faster Client Onboarding

Use AI to accelerate new client setup — auto-generate onboarding checklists, validate data, and reduce Go-Live TAT from weeks to days.

Smart Response Drafting

AI that reads the ticket, understands the context, and drafts a response that sounds like you — not a robot. Review and send in 30 seconds.

Customer Health Sensing

Build prompts that analyze customer interactions and flag early signs of churn or frustration — before the customer complains.

⚙️ Engineering & Product

AI-Assisted Debugging

Paste an error, get a fix. Use AI as your pair programmer — for code reviews, writing tests, explaining legacy code, and speeding up PRs.

Documentation Autopilot

Let AI write your docs, READMEs, and technical specs from your code. Spend time building, not explaining what you built.

📣 Growth & Marketing

Content at Scale

Generate ad copy variations, social posts, blog outlines, and campaign ideas in minutes. Test more, write less.

Performance Analysis

Paste your campaign data into AI, ask "what's working and what's not?" and get insights that would take hours of spreadsheet digging.

07 — Real Talk

Answering the questions you're thinking but not asking.

"I'm not technical. Is this for me?"
Especially for you. This program was designed for non-technical people. If you can type a message on WhatsApp, you can do this. Some of the best AI users at other companies are in Sales, HR, and Operations — not Engineering.

"I don't have 10 minutes."
Yes you do. You spent more than 10 minutes today on something that AI could have done in 30 seconds. This program will literally give you time back. The ROI on 10 minutes is hours saved per week.

"Will AI replace my job?"
No. But someone who knows how to use AI might. That's exactly why we're investing in this for everyone. The goal is to make you irreplaceable, not replaceable.

"What if I fall behind?"
The exercises are cumulative but forgiving. Miss a day? Do two the next day. Miss a week? Your buddy and your AI Champion will pull you back in. Nobody gets left behind.

"Is this mandatory?"
Let's put it this way: this is the company investing in your growth for free, on company time, with full support. The only question is why wouldn't you?
